# Convert to it!

**Truly universal online file converter.**

Convert to it! is a browser-based file conversion tool that breaks the boundaries of traditional converters. Unlike typical online tools that limit you to converting images to images or videos to videos, Convert to it! lets you convert between ANY formats - even unusual combinations like AVI video to PDF.

## Why Convert to it!?

### Privacy First
All conversions happen **entirely in your browser**. Your files never leave your device - no uploads, no servers, no tracking.

### Truly Universal
Convert between formats across different media types. Need to turn a video into a PDF? An image into audio? A font into an image? Convert to it! will try its best to make it work.

### No Barriers
- No account required
- No file size limits
- No watermarks
- Completely free and open source

### Powered by Industry Standards

Convert to it! leverages powerful open-source tools compiled to WebAssembly:

- **FFmpeg** - Video, audio, and multimedia conversion
- **ImageMagick** - Image processing and manipulation  
- **Pandoc** - Document format conversion
- And many more specialized handlers for niche formats
